Getting Assistance

Before You Contact Us

Our team aims to respond to all emails within one working day. For urgent issues during business hours, please call us directly. To help us resolve your issue as quickly as possible, please have the following information ready when you get in touch.

📋

Information to Have Ready

  • 1
    Your full name and staff role within the Doherty Cars system.
  • 2
    A clear description of the problem you are experiencing.
  • 3
    Any error messages that appeared on screen, including the exact wording where possible.
  • 4
    The steps you took before the problem occurred, so our team can try to reproduce the issue.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

6 topics covered
01I cannot log in to the system. What should I do?
+
Make sure you are entering your username and password correctly — both are case sensitive. If you still cannot log in, your account may have been marked as inactive. Contact your system administrator to check the status of your account.
02The Sell button on a vehicle is not available. Why?
+
A vehicle can only be sold after it has passed its inspection. If the inspection indicator is red, the vehicle has not yet been inspected. Ask a technician or admin to carry out the inspection first before attempting to sell the vehicle.
03I am receiving a permission error when performing an action.
+
Certain actions in the system are restricted based on your staff role. For example, only admins can add or deactivate staff accounts. If you believe your access level is incorrect, please contact your system administrator.
04Can a vehicle be sold more than once?
+
No. Once a vehicle has been sold its record is greyed out and the Sell button is disabled. The vehicle cannot be edited or sold again to preserve the integrity of the sales records in the system.
05Can a deactivated staff account be reactivated?
+
Yes. An admin can reactivate a deactivated account by locating the staff member in the Inactive Staff view, clicking their card, changing their status back to Active, and saving the changes.
06What formats can management reports be exported in?
+
Reports can be exported as PDF, Word, or Excel files. After generating a report, use the export icon at the top of the report viewer to select your preferred format.
